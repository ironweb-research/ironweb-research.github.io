# **Privacy Statement**
**Last Updated: April 5, 2025**

Thank you for choosing to be part of our community at **Ironweb** (“Company,” “we,” “us,” or “our”). We are committed to protecting your personal data and your right to privacy.  

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Statement or our data practices, please contact us via Telegram at [@ironweb](https://t.me/ironweb).

---

## **1. What Information Do We Collect?**

We may automatically collect certain information when you visit or interact with our services. This may include:

- IP address  
- Browser and device data  
- Usage behavior  
- Referrer URLs

> **Note:** We do **not** collect personally identifiable information unless you voluntarily submit it.

---

## **2. How Do We Use Your Information?**

We use your data for the following purposes, based on:

- **Legitimate interests**  
- **Contract performance**  
- **Legal compliance**  
- **Your consent**

### We may use your data to:

- **Enforce our terms and policies**
- **Fulfill orders, returns, and payments**
- **Request your feedback**
- **Send updates and policy changes**
- **Ensure security and prevent fraud**

---

## **3. Will Your Information Be Shared?**

Your data may be shared:

- **With your consent**
- **To fulfill contracts**
- **To comply with legal requests**
- **To protect safety and rights**
- **During business transfers (e.g., M&A)**

---

## **4. Is Your Data Transferred Internationally?**

Yes — your data may be processed or stored in **Taiwan**, **Singapore**, or other countries where our partners are based.  
We ensure your data remains protected with industry-standard safeguards.

---

## **5. How Long Do We Keep Your Data?**

We retain data **only as long as necessary** or required by law.

> **Typically: 1 month** after termination of your account unless legally required to retain it longer.

---

## **6. How Do We Keep Your Data Safe?**

We apply:

- Secure storage and encryption  
- Firewalls and access controls  
- Organizational data security practices  

> **However, no system is 100% secure**. Use our services in a secure environment.

---

## **7. What Are Your Privacy Rights?**

Depending on your country, you may:

- **Access your data**
- **Request deletion or correction**
- **Withdraw your consent**
- **Lodge a complaint with authorities**

📍 For EEA residents: [Contact your data authority](https://commission.europa.eu/law/law-topic/data-protection_en)

---

## **8. Account Access & Email Preferences**

You may:

- Review or update your account data  
- Request account termination  
- Unsubscribe from marketing emails via link or contact  

We may still contact you for **non-marketing purposes**.

---

## **9. How Can You Manage or Delete Your Data?**

Submit requests to your designated country officer or reach us via Telegram at [@ironweb](https://t.me/ironweb).  
We will handle requests per applicable data protection laws.

---

## **10. Will This Statement Be Updated?**

Yes. We may update this Privacy Statement to comply with laws or operational needs.  
Changes will be posted with a revised “Last Updated” date.
